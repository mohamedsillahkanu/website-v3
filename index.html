Who We Are

Informatics Consultancy Firm (ICF-SL) is Sierra Leone's premier data science and analytics consultancy, dedicated to transforming complex data into actionable business intelligence for governments, NGOs, and development partners.

Our multidisciplinary team of statisticians, data scientists, epidemiologists, and software engineers delivers cutting-edge solutions that drive evidence-based decision-making across West Africa.

Since our founding, we have built an outstanding reputation for rigorous methodology, innovative tools, and measurable impact — particularly in public health, national malaria control, and health systems strengthening.
